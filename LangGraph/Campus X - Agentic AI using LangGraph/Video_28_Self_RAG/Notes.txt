1. 3 main problems in RAG
    a. RAG retrieves even when retrieval is unnecessary

    b. RAG blindly trusts retrieved documents

    c. RAG doesn’t verify its own answers

2. Example:
    - User Query: "What causes diabetes?"

    - Vector DB/ Knowledge Base: "Diabetes is a chronic medical condition that affects how the body processes blood sugar (glucose)."

    - Even tho the user query answer's not same as the chunk present in vector DB, it will still be fetched and given to LLM for response and this is problematic
	
3. SELF RAG --> self reflection
	a. Self-RAG stands for Self Reflective RAG where the LLM actively judges its own retrieval, evidence, and answers instead of blindly trusting retrieved documents.
	
	Question it asks for self reflection
		i. Should retrieval happen?

		ii. Are the retrieved documents relevant?

		iii. Is the generated response grounded in the retrieved documents?

		iv. Does the response actually answer the user’s question?
